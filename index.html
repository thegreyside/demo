About Us

PT. Company Name is a national company located in Muara Badak or Marangkayu, East Kalimantan Indonesia. Our core business is mining support facilities which consist of stockpile, hauling road, coal crusher, conveyor, weightbridge, port and barge for every mining company around the location so that they don't have to build their own facilitation and instead use ours to deliver their product to the customer.

Our vision & mission

To be the largest mining support facilities ever built in Indonesia
